Who Are Saarloos Wolfdogs?

Long story short


The Saarloos Wolfdog is a unique and captivating breed created by crossing a German Shepherd with a Eurasian wolf. Known for their striking wolf-like appearance, these dogs are intelligent, independent, and highly loyal to their families. While reserved with strangers, they form strong bonds with their owners and thrive in active, experienced homes. Saarloos Wolfdogs require patience, proper training, and plenty of exercise, making them ideal companions for those who appreciate their wild spirit and gentle nature.

My pack consists of two incredible Saarloos wolfdogs: Baldur, a lively 3-year-old, and Fenrir, a wise and devoted 9-year-old. They thrive on training sessions, exploring nature, and being constant companions in everything I do. Whether we're hiking through forests or sharing quiet moments at home, their presence makes every day an adventure.
